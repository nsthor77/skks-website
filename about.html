Our Vision & Mission

The principles that guide everything we do

Our Vision

To be the leading bilingual school in Northeast Thailand, nurturing globally-minded students with strong values and academic excellence rooted in our unique Thai heritage.

📚

Our Mission

Provide world-class education through Cambridge-certified curriculum, qualified teachers, and a nurturing environment that develops the whole child — academically, socially, and emotionally.

💎

Our Values

Excellence · Integrity · Compassion · Innovation · Cultural respect · Lifelong learning · Community engagement · Sustainability

Heritage

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped SKKS

2011

Foundation

SKKS was founded with a vision to provide quality bilingual education in Khon Kaen — bringing world-class learning to Northeast Thailand.

2015

English Program Launch

Launched our flagship English Program for primary and secondary students, attracting families from across the region.

2018

Cambridge Certification

Received official Cambridge Assessment certification — a major milestone confirming our commitment to excellence.

2022

MEP Expansion

Expanded with the Mini English Program for students seeking balanced bilingual education with strong Thai cultural foundation.

2026

O-NET Perfect Score

Our students achieved a record-breaking O-NET 100% perfect score in English — a historic achievement for our school.
