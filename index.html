About SKKS

A School of Learning
for a Sustainable Future

"Suanson" represents the deeply rooted shade of pine trees, symbolizing our commitment to nurturing students with strong foundations.

🌟

Vision

To be a leading school in Northeastern Thailand that produces high-quality students with international standards.

🎯

Mission

Provide education emphasizing English and 21st-century skills.

💎

Values

SKKS — Smart, Kind, Knowledgeable, Successful

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents

Do students need to take an entrance exam?

The school uses interviews and readiness assessments.

What's the difference between EP and MEP?

EP teaches 70% in English. MEP blends Thai academics with English.

Is there school bus service?

Service covers Khon Kaen city area.

Are scholarships available?

Yes — for academic excellence, sports, and special talents.

What are the school hours?

Mon-Fri 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM, with after-school care available.

What documents are needed?

House registration, birth certificate, ID copies, photos, academic records.

Are there extracurricular activities?

Yes — music, sports, arts, Robotics, Coding, swimming, and more.

How is the cafeteria food?

FDA-certified, fresh ingredients cooked daily.
