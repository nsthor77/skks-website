Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SKKS

What is the difference between EP and MEP?

The English Program (EP) is full English immersion — Math, Science, English, and other subjects are taught entirely in English by native and certified teachers. The Mini English Program (MEP) is bilingual, with about 60% English instruction. Both programs are Cambridge-certified.

What ages and grades do you accept?

We accept students from Pre-Kindergarten (age 3) through Grade 9. Pre-K and Kindergarten are for ages 3-7, and EP/MEP programs run from Grade 1 to Grade 9.

How can I apply?

Simply visit our Admissions page to fill out the online application form. After submission, our admissions team will contact you within 2-3 business days to schedule an interview and assessment.

What are the tuition fees?

Tuition fees vary by program and grade level. Please visit our Admissions page for detailed fee structure. We also offer flexible payment plans.

Do you offer school bus service?

Yes! We provide school bus service to most areas in Khon Kaen city. Routes and fees are available upon request. Contact our office for more information.

What is the student-to-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio: 15:1 for Pre-K and Kindergarten, 20:1 for EP, and 25:1 for MEP. This ensures personalized attention for every student.

Are the teachers qualified?

Absolutely. All our teachers have at least a Bachelor's degree in Education or related field. Our EP teachers include native English speakers and Cambridge-certified educators.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Yes! We welcome campus visits. Please contact our office to schedule a tour. We can show you classrooms, facilities, and answer all your questions about life at SKKS.
